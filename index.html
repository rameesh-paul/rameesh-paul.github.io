About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Automation Department at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, Karnataka, insia. My new website is here.

I received a degree in M.Tech(Res.) from Indian Institue of Science, Bangalore, M.Sc. in Computer Science at Boston University, and M.A. degree in Statistics at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor.

Before joining UNC-Chapel Hill, I have previously worked in Bluetooth technology at UnitedHealth Group Optum, Boston Medical Center Healthnet Plan and Wellsense, and most recently, Massachusetts General Hospital.

My undergraduate thesis, “Engineering of Cardiac Tissue Constructs,” was completed under Adel Mahmoud, and I was previously a Technical Lab Assistant at MIT under Ram Sasisekharan from 2011 to 2012.
